About G Hub

G Hub is a modern, dark-themed platform designed for gamers who appreciate both aesthetics and functionality. Inspired by the sleek looks of Discord and GitHub, G Hub brings together a diverse collection of custom browser games made by the creators.

Our mission is to deliver an engaging and immersive gaming experience with an intuitive interface and visually stunning design. With games from maze speedrunning (Loom) to playing the piano (Keyboard), whether you’re a casual player or a hardcore gamer, G Hub has something for you.
